About Osmosis

Guardian College of Arts, Commerce, and Science proudly presents "Osmosis – The Flight of Hope," an annual career fair and inter-collegiate competition. The event draws its name from the biological process of osmosis, symbolizing the flow of knowledge into the minds of ambitious students. For the past five years, Osmosis has served as a bridge between the aspiring students of Kalyan and Dombivli and renowned institutions in Mumbai, guiding over 1,500 students toward their academic and professional goals. Osmosis not only connects students with educational opportunities but also offers a vibrant platform for them to showcase their talents in literature, performing arts, and visual arts. This year's edition, attracting around 2,000 attendees, features exciting new additions, including the Guardian Champion League, with sports events ranging from box cricket to e-sports like BGMI. With participation from over 100 schools and colleges, Osmosis 2024 promises to surpass all expectations, providing an unforgettable experience for all.
